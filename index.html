Gemologist Certification

Before purchasing a diamond, one must follow its documentation process, which proves that it has passed an impartial inspection, and obtain a "diamond certificate" (also known as a "gemological certificate"). The diamond certificate is supervised by a team of gemologists after a professional examination to determine the value of the diamond, measure its dimensions and check its properties under a microscope and other professional tools. The full report includes an analysis of the dimensions, color, finish, symmetry and other characteristics of the diamond. Many of the round diamond reports also include the polishing rating.
The institutes we work with - GIA, IGL, IGI, EGL, HRD
